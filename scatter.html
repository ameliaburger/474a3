Graduation Rates of High School Seniors on Free/Reduced Price Lunch (FRPL, Low Income)














This scatterplot visualizes how the graduation rate of high school seniors in Seattle and South King County who are on Free or Reduced Price Lunch (FRPL), which is an indicator of whether a student is low income, is influenced by the percentage of graduating seniors who took rigorous courses such AP, IB, or Cambridge courses. By mousing over a data point, you can see the school name, school district, and demographic information.
